khedma is a comprehensive web platform designed to connect customers with local technicians specializing in various home services. The platform allows customers to easily find qualified technicians and book their services, while enabling technicians to manage customer requests and showcase their services.
 Features
   For Customers
Browse Services: Explore various available home services
Find Technicians: Search for local technicians based on your location
Compare Prices: View inspection fees and ratings
Direct Booking: Book appointments with preferred technicians
Direct Contact: Contact technicians via phone

  For Technicians
 Dashboard: Easily manage booking requests
Status Updates: Accept or reject customer requests
Experience Showcase: Display years of experience and specialization
Profile Management: Update service information and pricing
